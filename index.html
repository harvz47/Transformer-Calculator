AUTOBOTS

The Autobots are the main protagonists in the universe of the Transformers, a collection of various toys, cartoons, films, graphic novels, and paperback books first introduced in 1984. The Autobots, led by Optimus Prime, are opposed by the Decepticons, headed by Megatron.

DECEPTICONS

The Decepticons are the main antagonists in the fictional universes of the Transformers multimedia franchise.They are depicted as a faction of sentient robotic lifeforms led by Megatron, identified by a purple face-like insignia.
